Prediction markets

1. Initial shares are divided among predefined options.

2. Share's price reflects probability of occurrence.

3. The winning option's shares become redeemable for $1, while all other shares become worthless.

Motivation:

- Monetary incentive to "update" a common data point when there's profit potential and

- Disincentive to misreport in the form of financial loss.

- Public odds act as reliable information source for the event
